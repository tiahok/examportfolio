FoodSharing Copenhagen

MAIN TASK

To create a campaign site that addresses one of the two main challenges the grassroots organization FoodSharing Copenhagen needs addressing: lack of voluntary help and running an effective campaign to incentivize people to use - instead of waste - the excess food they have.

A plethora of visual expression must be applied - also in the form of a video - to engage users and inspire them to receive the message.

The site should contain informational/educational and inspirational text, images and a video in a suitable layout and style.

PERSONAL ROLE

Concept and idea development. Storyboard creation and outline. Script writing and video treatment creation. Designing various illustrations for the website. Conceptualizing, writing and fine-tuning all the relevant copy and microcopy.

SKILLS GAINED

Learned about the different dramaturgical elements and storytelling techniques relevant to the creation of an effective campaign video. Learned the difference between various shots, the importance of composition, colours, and audio-visual effects.

Learned to work with cameras, manage lighting and use of audio equipment. Furthermore, aquired more deepened skills within the realm of sound and video editing, mostly through programs such as Adobe Premiere Pro and Adobe Audition.

Learned the importance of location scouting, planning different shots and angles, plus I have learned how to effectively distribute tasks and to ensure a smooth filming process.

Learned how to be critical, hitting certain emotional notes to deliver a clear, overarching message.
